Nutrition Values

Understanding the nutrients that power your body.

What Are Nutrition Values?

Nutrition values are the amounts of nutrients found in food. Understanding these values helps you make informed decisions about your diet and health. Key nutrients include carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals, all of which play vital roles in bodily functions.

Essential Nutrients

Carbohydrates

Provide energy for your body, essential for daily activities and exercise.

Proteins

Crucial for building and repairing tissues, and supporting immune function.

Fats

Provide long-term energy storage and help absorb fat-soluble vitamins.

Hydration and Its Importance

Water is essential for life and plays a key role in every bodily function. It helps transport nutrients, regulate temperature, and maintain cellular functions. Ensure to drink enough water daily for optimal health.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a balanced diet?

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods in the right proportions to provide the necessary nutrients for good health.

How can I improve my nutrition?

Focus on incorporating more whole foods, such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats into your diet.

What are micronutrients?

Micronutrients are vitamins and minerals that are essential for energy production, immune function, and bone health.
